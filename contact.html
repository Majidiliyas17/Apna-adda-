Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver within 5km radius of our location in Siddharth Nagar. Delivery charges may apply based on distance.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide catering services for events, parties, and corporate meetings. Contact us for customized menus and pricing.

What are your peak hours?

Our busiest times are mornings (7-10 AM) for chai and evenings (6-9 PM) for snacks. Pre-ordering is recommended during these hours.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have limited parking space available for our customers. Additional street parking is also available nearby.
