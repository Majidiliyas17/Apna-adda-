Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common franchise questions

What is the total investment required?

The total investment ranges from ₹8-35 Lakhs depending on the franchise model, location, and size of the outlet. This includes franchise fee, equipment, interior, and initial inventory.

How much space is required?

We recommend a minimum of 300-500 sq. ft. for a standard Apna Adda outlet. The space should have proper ventilation and necessary utilities.

What training is provided?

We provide comprehensive training covering food preparation, customer service, inventory management, and operations. Training duration is 2-3 weeks at our flagship store.

Is prior experience required?

No prior experience in food business is required. Our training program is designed to help first-time entrepreneurs succeed in the food industry.
